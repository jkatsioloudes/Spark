For Visionaries

Spark has a community of developers that are ready to take on just about any challenge. So if you've spotted a gap in the market or a problem that technology has not yet addressed and would like to see your vision come to life and benefit others, then you are in the right place! All you have to do is of what that vision or problem is and developers all around the world would be able to see it and work to provide a solution. But don't forget to share your vision/spark with your friends so that it can rise to the top and be more likely to catch the eye of a developer.

For Developers

With Spark those weeks without inspiration are gone! You as a developer would be able to find out what the general public really what for an app or website. Browse though the various problems/ideas that people have posted and find the ones that interest you or just provide us with enough details so that we can match you up with the best possible projects in accordance with your skills and interest.
